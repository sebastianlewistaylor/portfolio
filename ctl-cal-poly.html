Extended in-house design engagement for one of California's most progressive public university departments. The work spans symposia identities, event systems, instructional materials, and AI discussion frameworks — all operating within a formal institutional brand while finding room for precision and voice. Design as a function of academic culture: where the form of communication is itself an argument about what teaching can look like.
