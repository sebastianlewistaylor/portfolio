Digital Laboratory
Publishing as Infrastructure

The founding brand identity for a digital publishing platform built around the premise that academic knowledge should be globally accessible. Established through Cal Poly Humboldt Digital Commons, Digital Laboratory required identity work capable of holding institutional ambition — a mark, a system, a brand book designed to operate over time, not just at launch. Not a platform with a logo. An institution with a visual argument.
