Work Sebastian Taylor
Humboldt International Film Festival
Laurel, Redwood

The standard festival laurel signals international legitimacy. This one signals place. By substituting the olive branch with the Redwood — one of the last old-growth stands on earth — the identity becomes an act of ecological positioning as much as graphic design. A global format grounded in the specific weight of Humboldt County. Every application of the system is a quiet argument for where this festival belongs.
