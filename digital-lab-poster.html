Print as Provocation

Launching a digital publishing platform with a physical poster campaign is not irony — it's strategy. The visual language draws from vintage advertising: bold type, declarative copy, the confidence that assumes you already believe. Where new-media launches typically live inside the platforms they're disrupting, this campaign insisted on the street. A tangible argument for intangible infrastructure.
