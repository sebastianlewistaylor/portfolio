Tired of Resting

The album artwork was directed to engage the audience in a referential adventure into the life and times of Ijal. His music is heavily sample-oriented, lending itself to the historic textures of the album artwork.

Year
2023
Client
Ijal Morgan
Disciplines
Art Direction, Graphic Design, Video Production
Music Video — 2023

Utilizing solely archival footage, the teaser and music video was produced to coincide with the album drop. The historical images are all Creative Commons, sourced from the National Archives — moving images of MLK, John Baxter Taylor (the first African American to win an Olympic gold medal), among others. The project collectively amassed over 100,000 views and over 2,000 likes on Instagram.
